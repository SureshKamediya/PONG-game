Rules for multiplayer

  • First player will use w and s keys to move first wall above and below respectively.
  • Second player will use up and down arrow key to move second wall above and below respectively.

Easy level

The player who score 20 points first will win.

Medium level

The player who score 30 points first will win.

Hard level

The player who score 50 points first will win.

Rules for singleplayer

You have to use up and down arrow key to move first wall above and below respectively.

Easy level

If no of collisions will be less than 20 within time limit you will win.

Medium level

If no of collisions will be less than 30 within time limit you will win.

Hard level

If no of collisions will be less than 50 within time limit you will win.
